restaurant_menu The Ritual

1

Toast the Spirit of the Mole

Remove seeds and stems from the chilies. Toast them in a dry comal or heavy skillet until they are nearly blackened but not burnt. This deep roasting is the soul of the Oaxacan black mole.

2

The Grinding Process

Blend the toasted chilies with roasted tomatoes, garlic, and onions. Add the plantains and nuts. Ensure the texture is silky. If the blender struggles, add a splash of warm chicken or vegetable stock.

3

Infuse the Cacao

Simmer the mixture in a clay pot. Once it thickens, whisk in the dark chocolate chunks until fully melted. The sauce should become glossy and dark like midnight velvet.
